## About SBTi

The Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) is a corporate climate action organization that enables companies and financial institutions worldwide to play their part in combating the climate crisis.

We develop standards, tools and guidance which allow companies to set greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions reductions targets in line with what is needed to keep global heating below catastrophic levels and reach net-zero by 2050 at latest.

The SBTi is incorporated as a charity, with a subsidiary which will host our target validation services. Our partners are CDP, the United Nations Global Compact, the We Mean Business Coalition, the World Resources Institute (WRI), and the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF).

## Getting Started Guide for the SBTi FLAG Guidance

Use this diagram to determine the submission type your company needs to send to the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) to comply with the SBTi FLAG Guidance requirements.

### Decision Tree: Who Must Set a FLAG Science-Based Target?

**WHO must set a FLAG science-based target?**

A) The following sectors must set a FLAG target:
- Forest & Paper Products — Forestry, Timber, Pulp & Paper, Rubber
- Food Production — Agricultural Production
- Food Production — Animal Source
- Food & Beverage Processing
- Food & Staples Retailing
- Tobacco

B) Companies in other sectors must set a FLAG target if they have FLAG-related emissions totaling 20% or more of the company's overall emissions across scopes 1, 2 and 3.

Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise (SMEs) may submit their targets through the dedicated route for SMEs.

---

**Is your company required to set a FLAG science-based target?**

**→ NO:**

My company **chooses not** to set a FLAG target:
- Fill the information requested in the Corporate Target Submission Form, including FLAG information. All companies must account for FLAG-related emissions, even if they are not setting a separate FLAG target.

**OR**

My company **chooses to set** a FLAG target:
- It is recommended that companies with FLAG-related emissions that fall below the 20% threshold nonetheless set a FLAG target.

→ For companies that meet the FLAG-C1 criterion, a complete submission must include a FLAG target submission.

---

**→ YES:**

**Does your company have an existing near-term science-based target?**

Important notes (box in original):
- Companies must submit a FLAG target **within six months** after the release of the final version of the GHG Protocol Land Sector and Removals Guidance.
- Companies anticipating target recalculation for any reason are required to include FLAG targets upon (re)submission. This includes updating their existing targets or setting a net-zero target.

**→ YES (has existing near-term target):**

Does your company want to set a **net-zero target**?

- **No** → Near-term target update + FLAG target → *See next page*
- **Yes** → Near-term target update + Net-zero target + FLAG target → *See next page*

**→ NO (no existing near-term target):**

Does your company want to set a **net-zero target**?

- **Yes** → Near-term target + Net-zero target + FLAG target → *See next page*
- **No** → Near-term target + FLAG target → *See next page*

## Resumo dos pontos-chave

- Documento operacional de como iniciar o processo de submissão de FLAG targets
- Setores obrigados a setar FLAG target: Forest & Paper Products, Food Production (Agricultural/Animal Source), Food & Beverage Processing, Food & Staples Retailing, Tobacco
- Outros setores: obrigados se FLAG ≥ 20% das emissões totais (escopos 1+2+3)
- SMEs: rota dedicada separada
- Prazo crítico: empresas devem submeter FLAG target **dentro de 6 meses** após lançamento da versão final do GHG Protocol Land Sector and Removals Guidance
- Empresas com recálculo previsto devem incluir FLAG targets na (re)submissão
- Empresas que não setam FLAG target ainda assim devem reportar emissões FLAG no Corporate Target Submission Form
- 4 combinações possíveis de submissão (todas incluem FLAG Annex + SBTi FLAG Target-Setting Tool):
  1. Near-term update + FLAG
  2. Near-term update + Net-zero + FLAG
  3. Near-term + FLAG
  4. Near-term + Net-zero + FLAG
- Net-Zero requer adicionalmente o SBTi Net-Zero Tool
