## Critical Facts for Rapid Retrieval

### Key Criteria Numbers
- **Near-Term**: NT C1–C37 (GHG Accounting: C1–C7; Target Setting: C8–C37)
- **Net-Zero**: NZ C1–C37
- **SME Near-Term**: NZ C10–C37 + GHG C1–C7
- **Base year recalculation threshold**: >5% cumulative structural/methodological changes → NT C27

### Portal Submission Steps
1. Access form → 2. Choose service → 3. Complete submission → 4. Payment → 5. Sign T&Cs → 6. Validation in portal

### Validation Scenarios (S.1.1.1)
- Setting new SBTs
- Updating existing targets
- Setting additional SBTs

### Validation Services
- Near-Term | Net-Zero | Near-Term + Net-Zero | Near-Term + FLAG/Buildings | Near-Term + Net-Zero + FLAG/Buildings

### Contact Emails (SBTi Services)
- General/support: info@sbtiservices.com
- Registration: registration@sbtiservices.com
- Contract/payment: admin@sbtiservices.com

### CSV File Requirements
- **Company structure**: entity, description, share, allocation, scope1_2, scope3, justification
- **FLAG Commodity & Regional**: ghg_inventory, commodity, region, weight, intensity, methodology
- **FLAG Emissions Source**: ghg_inventory, commodity, luc, lm_co2, lm_non_co2, emissions_methodology, removals, removals_embedment, removals_methodology

### Emissions Allocation Exact Text (must match including punctuation)
1. `Scope 1 and 2, scope 3 categories 1 through 14`
2. `Scope 3 category 15`
3. `Excluded from scope 1 and 2, scope 3 categories 1 through 14`
4. `Excluded from scope 3 category 15`

### Supplier Engagement Targets
- Must be achieved within **5 years**
- Cannot be used for long-term net-zero targets
- Supplier scope 3 target required if supplier emissions ≥ 40% of company's total

### Five-Year Review Timeline
- Trigger: end of month 5 years after initial validation
- Form due: 6 months after trigger
- Targets due: 12 months after trigger

### Sector-Specific SDA Approaches in Portal
Automaker | Aviation SDA | Buildings in-use/upfront | Cement SDA | Land Transport SDA | Maritime SDA | Power SDA (own gen / all-sold) | Steel SDA | FLAG SDA (sector / commodity pathway)
