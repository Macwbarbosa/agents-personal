## Tier Eligibility — Tiered Pricing Model

SBTi Services uses a tiered pricing model based on annual turnover and organization type. The tier and organization type are determined at registration, during which the latest financial statement is reviewed.

Please direct questions about tier eligibility to registration@sbtiservices.com

## Discounts

Discounts are available based on annual turnover and country headquarters, as defined by the World Bank's income classification table. Companies must request a discount during registration to be considered and fee discounts will not be considered outside of the registration process. We encourage companies to pay the normal service fee if financially viable to ensure the discount can be reserved for companies that would benefit most from the discount.

## Services and Pricing

### 1.1 Service Descriptions

All corporate and FI target validation services include a technical review, validation call, and validation decision statement. Additional details can be found in the Standard Operating Procedure for the Validation of SBTi Targets.

**Corporate validation results are delivered within 40 business days** of the service start date, following the Validation Schedule. **FI validation results are delivered within 60 days** of the service start date. **SMEs receive a tailored validation service without a specific timeframe** for validation results.

### Table 3. Target Validation Service Descriptions

**Corporate Services:**

| Corporate Services | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Near-term | Validation of new near term targets |
| Net-zero | Validation of long-term, net-zero targets. Requires approved near-term targets. |
| Near-term and net-zero package | Discounted package price for validation of new near-term and net-zero targets together. The package rate is only available when originally submitted as a package. Amendments to service type will be charged the full standalone service fee. |
| Near-term and/or net-zero update | Streamlined update of existing near-term and/or net-zero targets. One fee covers validation for both near-term and net-zero updates. |
| Near-term update and net-zero package | Discounted package price for updating previously approved near-term targets and validating new net-zero targets together. The package rate is only available when originally submitted as a package. Amendments to service type will be charged the full standalone service fee. |
| FLAG and/or buildings | Companies may be required, or choose, to set FLAG (Forestry, Land, and Agriculture) target and/or buildings sector target(s). Refer to the SBTi FLAG Guidance and SBTi Buildings Criteria for more information on the type of entities which are required to submit these targets. This is an add-on validation and must be submitted with another service. One fee covers both FLAG and buildings targets. |
| FLAG and/or buildings update | Updates to approved FLAG and/or buildings targets. One fee covers both FLAG and buildings updates. |

**FI Services:**

| FI Services | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| FI Near-term | Validation of new near-term targets for FIs. |
| FI Net-zero | Validation of near-term and long-term, net-zero targets for FIs in line with the requirements of SBTi Financial Institutions Net-Zero Standard. |
| FI Near-term update | Streamlined update of existing near-term targets for FIs. |
| FI Buildings | Add-on validation of buildings targets for FIs. Must be submitted with another service. There is no additional fee to add buildings targets to FI services, *i.e. FI Near-Term and FI Net-zero validation fees are inclusive of this service.* |
| FI Buildings update | Add-on for updating approved buildings targets for FIs. Must be submitted with another service. There is no additional fee to add buildings targets to FI services, *i.e. FI Near-Term and FI Net-zero validation fees are inclusive of this service.* |

**SME Services:**

| SME Services | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| SME Near-term | Validation of new near term targets for SMEs. |
| SME Net-zero | Validation of long-term, net-zero targets for SMEs. Requires approved near-term targets. |
| SME Near-term and net-zero | Validation of new near-term and net-zero targets together. |
